About Me

I grew up in the suburbs of Chicago. I went to school for fine art. Some of my favorite artists include John Marin, Rene Magritte, Wasily Kandinsky, John Sargent and Mark Rothko. My hobbies include rock climbing, bouldering, painting, hiking, and swimming. I work for a company called Gatefeed and do graphic design work for Passport to Organics. Currently, I'm learning to become a web developer at Dev Bootcamp.
